HELLO SPRING

Hello, Spring, with sunlit breeze, Melting winter's frosted knees. Green shoots rise from slumber deep, Birds awake, their songs to keep.

Flowers peek from earthy hold, Blossoms unfold, stories told. Bumblebees in fuzzy dance, A gentle hum, a joyful trance.

The world reborn, a vibrant hue, Skies of blue, a canvas new. Hope unfurls on every bough, Spring has come, we breathe it now.
